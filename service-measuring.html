Free Flooring Measuring Service

Precision for the Perfect Fit

At Devlin Wholesale, we offer a free, no-obligation measuring service through our trusted network of local fitters, ensuring your new flooring is a perfect fit. By partnering with experienced professionals, we provide accurate, reliable measurements for homes and commercial spaces—saving you time, stress, and unnecessary expense while laying the groundwork for a flawless flooring installation.

We know that precision is key when it comes to fitting carpets, laminate, vinyl, or wood flooring. That’s why we take the guesswork out by measuring everything correctly from the start and offering tailored guidance based on your space layout and requirements.

Our Measuring Service includes:

  • Exact room measurements to ensure a precise, waste-free fit
  • Practical advice on floor layout, product suitability, and preparation needs
  • Flexible appointment scheduling at a time that works for you
  • Recommendations on next steps, for personalised flooring and accessory quotes

    • With meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to customer satisfaction, our measuring service gives you the confidence to move forward with your project — knowing everything has been planned for a smooth and seamless result.
