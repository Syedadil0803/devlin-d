Floor Care Services

Keep Your Flooring Looking Its Best

At Devlin Wholesale, we stock a wide range of professional-grade floor care kits and maintenance products to help you protect and prolong the life of your flooring. Whether you’ve chosen carpets, vinyl, laminate, or wood, our expert-recommended care solutions are designed to keep your floors looking fresh, clean, and beautiful for years.

Our Floor Care Services includes:

  • Specialist cleaning kits for vinyl, laminate, hardwood, and carpet.
  • Protective treatments to guard against stains, scratches, and daily wear.
  • Expert flooring advice maintenance tips, and cleaning solutions to keep floors looking great.
  • Advice from our flooring experts on how to maintain long-term finish and value.
  • Eco-friendly and gentle formulas that deliver a professional result without damaging your floor.

    • Whether you’re refreshing a new installation or maintaining an older surface, our floor care range offers convenience, reliability, and expert quality — all in one place. Visit our store for tailored product advice and keep your floors in showroom condition.
