The Ultimate Flooring Buying Checklist – What to Know Before You Visit a Store
April 05, 2019
  • by Harry Burns
  • 3 minutes read
Summary

Before setting foot in a flooring showroom, it pays to be prepared. This blog walks you through everything you should know—from measuring your space to comparing materials—so you can shop smarter and feel confident in your decision.

Flooring isn’t just a purchase—it’s an investment in your home’s style, comfort, and resale value. But walking into a store without a plan can feel overwhelming. With endless choices, materials, finishes, and price points, how do you know what’s right for your home and budget?

This ultimate pre-shopping checklist ensures you arrive informed, focused, and ready to find the perfect flooring.

🧠 1. Know Your Priorities

Before browsing samples, ask yourself :

• 🏡 Which room(s) are you updating?
• 👣 Do you need softness, durability, or water resistance?
• 💰 What’s your budget (including fitting)?
• 🎨 What colour schemes and styles work with your space?

📏 2. Measure Before You Go

Save time and get more accurate advice by measuring your space in advance.

• 📐 Bring rough dimensions (length × width) for each room.
• ✏️ Note awkward shapes, steps, or obstacles.
• 📸 Snap a few pictures of your rooms to show the salesperson.

🆚 3 .Understand the Flooring Types

Be familiar with the pros and cons of each material:

• 🧸 Carpet: Warm and soft, great for bedrooms and stairs
• 💧 LVT: Durable, waterproof, ideal for kitchens and bathrooms
• 🪵 Laminate: Stylish and affordable, perfect for living areas
• 🌿 Artificial Grass: Low-maintenance green space for balconies and garden rooms
• 🪚 Engineered Wood: High-end look with lasting value

🧰 4. Ask These In-Store Questions

Don’t leave without the answers to these:

• Is underlay required or included?
• Is the product suitable for underfloor heating?
• What’s the wear warranty or guarantee?
• What’s the lead time for fitting or delivery?

📦 5. Plan Beyond the Floor

Think beyond the product:

• 🚚 Ask about delivery and installation services
• 🧹 Check what prep is needed (e.g. old flooring removal)
• 🛠️ Enquire about care products or accessories
• 🧾 Understand the full cost breakdown—no surprises!
