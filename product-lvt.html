Luxury Vinyl Tile

At Devlin Wholesale, we specialise in Luxury Vinyl Tile (LVT), offering a stunning flooring solution that combines elegance with practicality. Perfect for both residential and commercial spaces, LVT mimics the beauty of natural wood or stone while providing exceptional durability and easy maintenance. It’s water-resistant, scratch-resistant, and designed to withstand the rigours of everyday life.

Our Luxury Vinyl Tile services include:

  • A diverse selection of styles, colours, and textures to suit your décor
  • Expert guidance on choosing the right LVT for your specific needs
  • Professional measuring and fitting services to ensure a seamless installation
  • Care tips to help you maintain the beauty of your new flooring

    • Transform your space with the luxurious look of vinyl tiles that provide comfort and resilience. Let our knowledgeable team assist you in finding the perfect flooring solution that elevates your home or business!
