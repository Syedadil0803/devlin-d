Solid & Engineered Wood

At Devlin Wholesale Leicester, we specialise in high-quality Solid and Engineered Wood flooring that brings warmth and elegance to any space. Our extensive range includes various styles and finishes, allowing you to find the perfect match for your home or business. Solid wood offers a timeless appeal, while engineered wood provides enhanced stability and moisture resistance, making it ideal for diverse environments. or stone.

Our Solid & Engineered Wood services include:

  • Expert advice to help you choose the right flooring for your lifestyle
  • Professional measuring and fitting services for a seamless installation
  • Guidance on care and maintenance to keep your wood flooring looking beautiful
