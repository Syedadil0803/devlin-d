About Devlin

Devlin Wholesale Carpets is an independent family-run business with years of experience in supplying carpet and flooring to the public, trade, and landlords. We are proud to offer a wide range of flooring at wholesale prices, including carpets, laminate, vinyl, LVT, and carpet tiles, to suit every budget and need.

We work with the biggest brands in the flooring industry to bring our customers the best deals and quality. Our large warehouse is open to the public and displays hundreds of rolls and roll ends in stock and ready to take away or have professionally fitted.

At Devlin, we take great pride in our deep knowledge of flooring products and our personalized customer service. Whether you're renovating a single room, outfitting an entire property, or working on a commercial space, our experienced team is here to guide you every step of the way. We believe in making premium flooring accessible to everyone—without compromise.

Our business thrives on repeat customers and referrals, a testament to our unwavering commitment to value, trust, and satisfaction. From selection to installation, we aim to make your experience smooth, affordable, and inspiring.

Our Mission: Bringing Value & Comfort to Every Home

“At Devlin Wholesale, we are dedicated to delivering unbeatable value by offering excellent choice, top-quality carpets, and outstanding service at wholesale prices. Our goal is to transform every home with comfort, style, and lasting value.”

Our Vision: Shaping the Future of Flooring

To be the most trusted and recognized carpet and flooring destination in Leicestershire and beyond. We strive to reshape customer perceptions by offering excellent choice, quality, value for money, and service. As we grow, we will continue to set new standards in affordability and quality, becoming the go-to choice for every home and business.

Our Commitment to Flooring Excellence

Our Goal: To provide every customer with the perfect flooring solution whether premium or budget-friendly—through transparent pricing, competitive wholesale rates, and exceptional service. We aim to ensure unbeatable value, quality, and confidence in every purchase by focusing on:

• Offering a wide selection of high quality flooring
that meets diverse tastes and functional needs.

• Providing expert guidance
to help customers make informed decisions that suit their style and budget.

• Maintaining clear, honest communication
throughout the buying and fitting process to build trust and avoid confusion.

• Delivering consistent customer satisfaction
that encourages referrals, repeat business, and long term loyalty.

• Continuously improving
our product offerings and service experience based on customer feedback and the latest industry trends.

Our Core Values: What Drives Us

1. True Value Commitment
We provide the best possible quality at unbeatable wholesale prices, ensuring every customer gets exceptional flooring without unnecessary markups or misleading discounts.

2. Customer-First Approach
Every decision we make is centered around the customer—understanding their needs, guiding them to the best solutions, and ensuring a seamless buying experience.

3. Trust Through Transparency
We build long-term relationships by being honest about our products, pricing, and services—ensuring customers make informed decisions without pressure.

4. Uncompromising Quality & Service
We uphold the highest standards in our products and service, ensuring every carpet we sell meets durability, style, and performance expectations.
