About Devlin Wholesale
Flooring Made Affordable, Personal, and Hassle-Free

At Devlin Wholesale, we’re more than just a flooring supplier—we’re a family-run business built on trust, expertise, and genuine care for our customers. Based in Leicester, Tamworth, Minworth, Lichfield we’ve spent years helping homeowners, landlords, and tradespeople find the perfect flooring at unbeatable wholesale prices.

From plush Carpets to stylish Laminate, hardwearing Vinyl Rolls, modern LVT, and versatile Carpet Tiles, we offer a wide selection of top-brand flooring to suit every taste, budget, and lifestyle.

Why Choose Devlin Wholesale?

✅ Independent & Family-Run – Honest service, real people, no pressure
✅ Huge In-Stock Selection – Hundreds of rolls and roll ends ready to take away or fit
✅ Open to the Public & Trade – Flooring made accessible for all
✅ Expert Knowledge – Decades of experience to guide your every decision
✅ Tailored Advice – We take time to understand your space and needs
✅ Competitive Prices, Everyday – Get more for your money without compromising on quality

Our Showrooms are open to everyone—walk in, browse freely, and speak to our friendly team who are always ready to help, not to sell. Whether you’re renovating a single room or refitting a full property, we’re here to make flooring simple, affordable, and even enjoyable.

Most of our customers come back or refer us to others—and that’s the biggest compliment we could ask for. We believe in building long-term relationships through great service, trusted products, and honest pricing.

Let Devlin Wholesale help you transform your space with flooring that fits your style, your needs, and your budget.

Our Mission

Every home deserves comfort, style, and lasting quality, and we’re here to make that transformation simple, affordable, and stress-free.
