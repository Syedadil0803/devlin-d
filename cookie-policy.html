Cookie Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Devlin Wholesale uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy — subject to your cookie preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. They help websites function efficiently and provide useful information to website owners.

Some cookies are essential to make the site work properly, while others help us understand how visitors interact with our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function correctly. They enable basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information.

2. Performance & Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use our site. These cookies help us understand:

  • Which pages are most visited
  • How users move around the site
  • How long visitors stay on pages

    • The data collected is aggregated and anonymous — it does not personally identify you. To learn more about how Google handles data, visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

    Managing Cookies

    You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

    • View what cookies you have and delete them
    • Block third-party cookies
    • Block all cookies from being set
    • Clear all cookies when you close your browser

    Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect how the website functions.

    To find out how to manage cookies in your browser, use the following links:

    • Mozilla Firefox
    • Apple Safari
    • Microsoft Edge

    To find out how to manage cookies in Google Chrome browser, follow the steps below:

    • Google Chrome – Open Chrome, go to Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data

    Future Changes to Cookies

    As our website grows and more features are added (e.g. online store or customer tools), we may use additional cookies. When that happens, this policy will be updated to reflect those changes.
